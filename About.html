About

A brief history of Hydr throughout the years.

2013

Hydr is a singer songwriter based in London. He began performing solo in 2011 at small venues around central London. Always wanting to better himself within audience communication, at the end of 2013, Hydr began performing fewer scheduled shows and took his music and performances to the streets of London.

2013/2014

During 2013 and 2014 Hydr street performed throughout Europe as well as keeping strong ties to London. Around this time Hydr also worked as a Session Musician, gaining experience working with producers and other artists in a studio setting.

2015

This led to a decision of pushing forward with his own projects in the studio. By the end of 2014, Hydr began working on his long-awaited self-produced debut album "Orange Sky"

2016

By 2016 Hydr’s fan base had grown substantially and not wanting to rush his full album he saw a 4 track EP as a medium to keep his fan happy but not sacrifice the quality of his work. Named (“The Busking Life EP") it is due for release on Sunday the 28th of May.

2017

At the start of March Hydr formed a band consisting of Mohsin Hanid, Aléxandros Alfa, Cesare Giorgini, Daniele Montanari. They began rehearsing The Busking Life EP along with other tracks from Hydr’s album "Orange Sky" in preparation for an Autumn Tour.

A behind the scenes documentary on the making of The Busking Life EP and the formation of the band launched in March. This will be released alongside the EP with an additional music video.

You can always find Hydr performing on the street of central London every weekend.
